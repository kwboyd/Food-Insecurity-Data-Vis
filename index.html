Food Security in the U.S.

By KATE BOYD

OCTOBER 28, 2015

More than one in ten Americans qualify as "food insecure." Those who are food secure are certain of their ability to purchase balanced meals at all times of the year, while food insecurity is defined as "limited or uncertain availability of nutritionally adequate and safe foods" by the United States Department of Agriculture.
